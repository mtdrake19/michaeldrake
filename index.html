ABOUT THE THE WINDY CITY!

Chicago is one of the world's greatest places to visit. As you browse through, I hope you are amazed by the history, culture, and activities Chicago has to offer.

There are many attractions in Chicago ranging from food to culture. However, the main attraction, and the first stop of 99% of tourists that first come here is the Willis Tower. Originally named the Sears Tower, you have the option to literally hover over the city on a glass surface. Scared of heights? That's alright, because you can be grounded and still discover the best of Chicago in Navy Pier. With mini golf, cruise ships, and playlands for children, the option for entertainment seems endless.

As a visitor or local resident, don't forget to check out the local concerts and festivals that goes on such as Lalapalooza and the Taste of Chicago.

Above, you see one of the most attention grabbing sites in Chicago; the Chicago Bean. This was created by British artist, Anish Kapoor. If you're an artist, take notes! As this is one of the many pieces in Chicago that will inspire you to keep chasing your dreams.
